MARS:

Picturesque and
closer than ever

In 2022 our spacecraft BFR (Big Falcon Rocket) will head towards the Red Planet, Mars. As of today only 18 missions have been successful, however, we are about to change that. In 2022 our spacecraft filled with cargo is ready for take-off, with a mission to establish resources and sort out initial power, mining and infrastructure.

2024 is the year our spacecraft will include people, alongside cargo. This time, to build a propellant depot and our first base on Mars. We are now preparing for a future cosmopolitan society on our neighbour, the Red Planet.
